# StickerBoard Contact

## How to Contact Us

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests related to StickerBoard, please reach out using the method below.

## Contact

- **Contact Form**: [Contact us here](https://forms.gle/Ngx3Fq5XnZJKxAaD6)

## Information to Include

To help us respond smoothly, please include the following information:

### For Bug Reports
- Your iPhone model (e.g., iPhone 15)
- iOS version (e.g., iOS 18.0)
- App version (available on the Settings screen)
- Description of the issue (what action you took and what happened)
- Screenshot if possible

### For Feature Requests and Feedback
- Please describe your request as specifically as possible

## About Subscriptions (Pro Plan)

### Inquiries About Purchases and Payments
Subscription purchases and payments are processed through Apple's App Store. For payment-related inquiries, please contact Apple Support.

- [Apple Support](https://support.apple.com/)

### How to Cancel
1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name (Apple ID)
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "StickerBoard"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

### Refunds
Subscription refunds are subject to Apple's policies. To request a refund, please visit [Apple's Report a Problem page](https://reportaproblem.apple.com/).

## Response Times

- Responses to inquiries may take several days
- As this is an independently developed app, we may not be able to respond to all inquiries, but all feedback will be taken into consideration for future development
